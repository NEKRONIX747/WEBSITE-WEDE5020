About Us

History of Our Business

This business was created out of passion and love for the art we call baking. Our business was established in 2019 where it first started as Bakeables, a successful family-run bakery in Hayfields, Pietermaritzburg. Covid-19 caused us to temporarily close, but our passion remained, leading to the creation of The Littlest Homestead, working tirelessly from our kitchen to bring you the most delectable delights.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to bring joy and happiness through our baked goods. We hope to bring smiles and fulfillment with every order, aiming to grow our business and share the delight our products bring. One day, we hope to establish a successful bakery where people can enjoy delicious delights at affordable prices.
