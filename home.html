Welcome to The Littlest Homestead

History

Founded out of passion and love for the art of baking, our company was established in 2019 as Bakeables. Though COVID-19 forced us to pause, our dedication never wavered. We now proudly operate as The Littlest Homestead, delivering the most delectable delights from our family-run kitchen.

Mission

Our mission is to bring joy and share happiness through our baked goods, spreading smiles and fulfillment with every order. We aim to grow our business and one day open a local bakery offering delicious treats at affordable prices.
